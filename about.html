About SEAL

Sea, Air, Land Enterprises, LLC (SEAL) was founded on the belief that travel is more than movement—it’s about connection. From retirees seeking comfort, to families making memories, to affinity groups sharing passions, and teams celebrating success, SEAL designs journeys that matter.

With 50+ years of combined travel expertise and partnerships with trusted organizations like Fora Travel, we go beyond booking trips—we anticipate needs, simplify logistics, and deliver unforgettable experiences.

Our Mission & Values

  • Connection: Every journey is designed to bring people closer.
  • Care: Anticipatory service that removes stress and adds joy.
  • Expertise: Decades of knowledge and trusted partnerships.
  • Inclusivity: Experiences tailored for all ages, abilities, and interests.
  • Excellence: Precision planning and white-glove support from start to finish.

Partners & Associations

  • Fora Travel – Trusted partner for global travel logistics.

  • IATA (International Air Transport Association) – Air travel expertise.
